How to Play

Piece together words using the letters provided. The longer the word the higher the score.

Click the submit button to enter a word. The current entry will appear under the words section on the left.

Clicking shuffle will replace all letters on the board.

Only words appearing in the english dictionary will be considered valid submissons. No points will be rewarded for non-words or typos.

There are only 30 seconds in each game.
